Timely

A different kind of clock.

  • ← See all work

Timely was a different take on an alarm clock. Instead of looking at your phone to read the time, Timely tells you the time.

This app would be helpful for both pros and regular folk alike. If you’re a music teacher or counselor, you can get a helpful reminder when your time’s up. You could also get a reminder that it’s time to wake your kid up from a nap, all without having to worry about looking at your phone.

We wanted to put people directly into the action, so we stripped everything away that didn’t matter. Putting functionality first was our goal—people are busy and they just want their apps to work. Seemed reasonable to us, at least.

Of course, that didn’t mean the interface would be boring. This app was designed for iOS 6 (before it stopped being cool to have any textures) and had a much lighter visual touch to it. Bright, soothing colors, smooth typography, and obvious iconography helped people focus more on what matters (their activities) and less about what didn’t (spending time in our app).
