Concussion Manager

Diagnosing sports concussions in a fraction of the time.

  • ← See all work

Built with Capacity Sports (now Sway Medical), we found a way to diagnose concussions on the field in a fraction of the time. This had rigorous testing and evaluation, and Chase Curtiss led the way on that. I worked with them to help visualize what certain kinds of tests would look like. We worked on memory recall, balance, and reaction time. While we focused on youth soccer, the application was much broader and could work for any sport.

We worked to establish baselines for each person by having them take the test during practice or another time when they weren’t likely to receive a concussion. This helped us find out if there was a problem later during a game. People received a detailed evaluation and score with a recommendation on whether or not they could continue playing.

There was also a content management system that we created to help administrators, coaches, and parents to manage and view their data.
